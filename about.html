About Japan OTAKU Insider

Welcome to Japan OTAKU Insider! This is your ultimate English database for Japanese otaku culture, tailored specifically for overseas otaku.

What We Offer

We aggregate the latest news, announcements, and limited-time events from Japan across 7 categories:

  • Collab Cafes: Pop-up restaurants and themed cafes featuring your favorite anime and game characters.
  • Figures: New announcements covering Nendoroids, scale figures, prize figures, and model kits.
  • Events: Anime conventions, doujinshi markets, and special exhibitions in Japan.
  • Anime News: Breaking updates on new series, manga adaptations, and broadcast schedules.
  • VTuber: Virtual YouTuber news, events, and collaborations.
  • Game News: Japanese game releases, updates, and gaming culture.
  • OTAKU NEWS: General otaku culture news that spans multiple categories.

Data Sources & Updates

We pull from 27+ Japanese RSS feeds (including Natalie, 4Gamer, Amiami, MoguLive, and more) and supplement with AI-powered search to ensure comprehensive coverage. Our database is updated automatically every day.
