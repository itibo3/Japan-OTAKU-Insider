Details (EN)

The DAY1 performance of the event "'Nintama Rantaro' Special Event Gratitude Gathering Sakura Festival 2026" of the anime "Nintama Rantaro" based on the original work by Sobei Amago will be held on April 10th at LINE CUBE in Tokyo. Held at SHIBUYA (Shibuya Public Hall), Minami Takayama as Rantaro Inaji, Mayumi Tanaka as Kirimaru of Settsu, Sadatomo Ichiryusai as Shinbei Fukutomi, Toshihiko Seki as Hansuke Doi, Ushio Ken Narita who played the role of Bunjiro E, Soichiro Hoshi who played the role of Senzo Tachibana, Shigeru Shibuya who played Nagaji Nakazai, Ennen Kana who played the role of Koheita Nanamatsu, Ryotaro Okiayu who played the role of Isaku Zenpoji, and Chihiro Suzuki who played the role of Ruzaburo Shokumitsu. Amago also made a special appearance.
