Bandai has started accepting reservations for the electronic toy ``Kingdom Hearts Keyblade Kingdom Chain Dark Side'' on the mail order site ``Premium Bandai'' from 4:00 pm on April 30th. Shipping is scheduled for October, and the price is 9,900 yen.
