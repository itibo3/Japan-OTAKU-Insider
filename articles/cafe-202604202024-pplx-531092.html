Details (EN)

A limited-time collaboration between the popular manga “Hozuki no Reitetsu” by Natsumi Eguchi and Karaoke no Tetsujin will be held from April 24th to June 7th, 2026. Collaboration menus and goods inspired by Hozuki, Shiro, and Incense will be available, and a mini photo-style card will be randomly presented with each collaboration drink ordered. We also have concept rooms available only at participating stores.
