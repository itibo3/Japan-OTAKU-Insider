Details (EN)

``Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba the Movie: Mugenjou Chapter 1: Return of the Myriad'' is currently in theaters. It has recently been revealed that the film's box office revenue exceeded 40.13 billion yen in the 262 days of release until April 5, 2026. As the movie is about to end its run on April 9th, excluding some theaters, it is only a few hundred million yen away from the previous movie, ``Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba the Movie: Mugen Train'', which made 40.75 billion yen.
