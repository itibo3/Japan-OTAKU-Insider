The BitSummit Executive Committee today announced the streamers and creators who will participate as official PR supporters at the indie game event "BitSummitPUNCH" to be held at Kyoto Miyako Messe from May 22, 2026. At the special booth, developers and designers will be invited as guests, and talk sessions will be held where they will hear behind-the-scenes stories about development, as well as live play sessions and more.
