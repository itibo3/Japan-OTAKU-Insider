A collaboration cafe celebrating the 30th anniversary of manga artist Arina Tanemura's art career will be held at Animate Cafe Ikebukuro 3rd store/Nagoya 2nd store from April 17th to May 17th, 2026. For every order from the collaboration menu, a random coaster with a newly drawn illustration (10 types in total) will be presented as a bonus, and limited goods will also be on sale.
