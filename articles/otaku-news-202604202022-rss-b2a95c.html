Details (EN)

Kiritappu Onsen Yuyu, a day-trip hot spring facility in Hamanaka Town, Hokkaido, which is revitalizing the town with the theme of Lupine the Third, has rebranded and opened on April 20, 2026, with the official name changed to Lupine the Third Hot Spring Kiritappu Onsen Yuyu. The collaboration that has been ongoing in areas such as decorations and merchandise sales has been completely deepened, and the entire facility has been reborn into the world of Lupine the Third, starting with a new logo, bathrooms, BGM, exhibitions, and merchandise.
