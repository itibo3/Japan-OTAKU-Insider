SFC's masterpiece ``Super Turrican'' is back as a cartridge with the addition of various elements that were abandoned at the time. Columbus Circle has announced that it will release "Super Turrican & DIRECTOR'SCUT" for SFC/SFC compatible machines in late July 2026. Tetsuhiko Kikuchi, known for works such as ``Guardian Heroes'', created the package illustration.
