Details (EN)

The short anime ``I Love the Earth! Kikkun'', which is based on the original work and character designs by Yuichi Nakamaru, will be broadcast on ``Gojiki Mutsu!'' from July. Nakamaru plays the role of the main character, Kikkun. Yoshino Nanjo, Akio Otsuka, Tomokazu Sugita, Sougo Nakamura, Emi Nitta, and Azusa Tadokoro will also appear.
