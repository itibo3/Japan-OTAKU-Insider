Introducing men's T-shirts by PLST, a brand that proposes ``everyday clothes'' when you want to look neat, with motifs of ``Dada the Three-Faced Monster'' and ``Breton the Four-Dimensional Monster'' from ``Ultraman.'' It has been revealed that it will be available in stores nationwide and online from late May 2026. There will also be an EC-only complete set that includes an original tote bag.
