Details (EN)

To commemorate the 20th anniversary of Capcom's Japanese-style fantasy RPG "Okami" series, a collaboration cafe will be held at Monster Hunter Bar Tokyo/Osaka from April 20th to June 1st, 2026. Menus with Amaterasu and Princess Sakuya motifs will be available, and for every item ordered from the collaboration menu, customers will receive one original coaster at random from a total of eight types.
