Details (EN)

It has been revealed that ``Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba the Movie: Mugenjou Chapter 1: The Second Coming of the Myriad'' has attracted 27,455,968 viewers and box office revenue of 40,219,000 yen during its 266 days of release until April 9, 2026. In addition, it was revealed that by the same day in the world, including Japan, the cumulative audience attendance was 98,520,310 people, and the total box office revenue was 117,917,539,329 yen (*Monthly results (USD) converted to yen using the monthly average mid-price).
