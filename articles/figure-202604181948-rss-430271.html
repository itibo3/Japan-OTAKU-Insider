Details (EN)

The Zoukei-Mura SWS (Super Wing Series) developed by Volks is a series of airplane model plastic kits produced by the Zoukei-Mura brand, Japan's only modeling department that produces and sells three-dimensional objects. Among them, there is a particularly large lineup of German military aircraft developed during World War II, and various aircraft such as "1/32 Messerschmitt Bf 109 G-14/U4 "Erich Hartmann"" and "1/32 Horten Ho 229 B (two-seat type)" have been made into three-dimensional models.
