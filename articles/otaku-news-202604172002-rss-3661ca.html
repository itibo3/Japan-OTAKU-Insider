Details (EN)

The first official recipe book from "Apothecary's Hitorigoto", "Cat and Cat's Recipe", where you can enjoy the dishes in the story, is now available. It will be released on May 13, 2026, with recipes for many of the dishes that captured the hearts of readers in the story, including the chocolate made by Maomao in response to Jin's request, ``Could you please make me an aphrodisiac?'' and the frozen confections that are proof of his friendship with Xiaoran and Kosui, under the full supervision of the original author Natsu Hyuga.
