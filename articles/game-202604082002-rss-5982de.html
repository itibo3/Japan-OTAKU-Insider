Details (EN)

Hamster announced today that distribution of the arcade archives version of ``Polaris'' will begin on April 9, 2026. Polaris is an STG released by Taito in 1980. Players control a submarine and fight against enemy forces using missiles that can only be fired directly overhead. Face off against attacks from all directions, including bombing from the air, frigates on the surface of the sea, and enemy submarines underwater.
