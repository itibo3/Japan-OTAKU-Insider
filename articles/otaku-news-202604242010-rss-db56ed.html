A collaboration project for the Nintendo Switch 2 software "Pokoa Pokemon" will be implemented at approximately 16,400 FamilyMart stores nationwide from April 28, 2026. Under the theme of "Play with Pokemon at Family Mart," original products will be sold, including frappes with Pikachu and Metamon designs, and original keychains will also be given to purchasers of eligible products.
