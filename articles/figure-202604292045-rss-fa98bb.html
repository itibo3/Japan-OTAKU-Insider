Details (EN)

Bandai will release candy toys ``SO-DO CHRONICLE Kamen Rider G7 & Kamen Rider G3-X Set'' and ``SO-DO CHRONICLE Kamen Rider G6 Set'' in November. Reservations are scheduled to start on Premium Bandai from 13:00 on April 29th, with prices ranging from 7,260 yen for the Kamen Rider G7 & Kamen Rider G3-X set to 5,995 yen for the Kamen Rider G6 set.
