Rob Studio announced on April 4, 2026 that the new title "Game Shop Shayo", which is currently under development, will be released on Steam on April 22. This work is a management simulation game that allows you to relive the changes in the game industry over a period of about 25 years from 1985 to 2010 from the perspective of managing a game shop.
