Details (EN)

The experiential exhibition ``Baribari Densetsu VR RIDE'', which recreates the manga ``Baribari Densetsu'' and combines an actual CB750F and the latest VR simulator, is being held at ``Bikers Paradise Minami Hakone'' in Kannami-cho, Shizuoka Prefecture from April 24, 2026. This is a joint project between Bike King & Company and Prototype, allowing you to experience immersing yourself in the world of the original work.
