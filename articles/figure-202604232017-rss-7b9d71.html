Japanese title

流水のように麗しいアクションフィギュア「海霧の精～マフィネ～」スタジオソータから8月発売

Summary

Studio Sota will release the action figure ``Sea Mist Spirit ~Muffine~'' by sculptor isasa as a box toy in late August. The price is 1,980 yen for "Muffine" alone and 4,180 yen for "Sea Fog Spirit & Muffine Clear Color Set."
