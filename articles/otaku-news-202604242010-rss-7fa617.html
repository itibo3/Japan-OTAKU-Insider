Details (EN)

It has been announced that the project ``Maomao's Pharmacy University Exploration'', which is a collaboration with the TV anime ``Hitorigoto of the Pharmacist'' and conveys the depth and fun of Chinese medicine, will be held at the ``3 advanced Chinese herbal medicine universities'' that have Departments of Chinese Herbal Medicine/Chinese Medicine Courses: Yokohama Pharmaceutical University, Nippon Pharmaceutical University, and Daiichi Pharmaceutical University. A special video is currently being released, and an experiential collaboration project will be held at the open campuses that will be held at three universities starting April 25th.
