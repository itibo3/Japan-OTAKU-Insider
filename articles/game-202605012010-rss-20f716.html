Details (EN)

Kairosoft began distributing the Switch 2/Switch version of "Airship Quest Island" on April 30, 2026. This work is a simulation RPG set on islands floating in the sky, where you go on an adventure in an airship. The player takes on the role of a new captain, developing the island and solving the problems of the travelers. Use the money you earn to strengthen your ship and crew and aim for further islands.
