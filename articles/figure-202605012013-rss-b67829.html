Hobby Japan's figure brand ``AMAKUNI'' will release the figure ``Swimsuit Silhouette Date A Live Kurumi Tokisaki Competitive Swimsuit Ver.'' from September to October. The reservation period is until June 16th. The price is 19,800 yen.
