On April 7th, Space Entry announced the start of the ``HELLO, HARO'' project at ``Inagi Transmission Base Pear Terrace'' in Inagi City, which will assemble ``Everyone's Haro,'' an autonomous space robot designed by Kunio Okawara based on the pet robot ``Haro'' from ``Mobile Suit Gundam,'' and launch it to the International Space Station (ISS) for a long-term stay in the Japanese Experiment Module ``Kibo.''
