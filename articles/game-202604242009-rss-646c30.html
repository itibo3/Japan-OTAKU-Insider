bilibili will start pre-registration for the new smartphone RPG "Ruumi Master" on April 24, 2026. It features hand-drawn graphics that look like a moving picture book, and in addition to capturing and raising hundreds of monsters called ``Ruumi'', it also includes home management and mini-games where you can freely create your own space. Global release is scheduled for summer 2026.
