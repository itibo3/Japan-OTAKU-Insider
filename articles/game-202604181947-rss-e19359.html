Details (EN)

On April 18, 2026, TheWanderingBand and StrayFawnPublishing ended early access for "Airborne Empire" and released the official version "1.0". The price is 3400 yen (tax included). This work is an RPG that lets you explore a vast open world while managing a city floating in the sky. The official version adds new elements such as a snow-covered biome and a jet engine that enables high-speed flight.
