Details (EN)

HeadupGames and BeepJapan today announced that the PC (Steam) version of the narrative FPS adventure game "INDUSTRIA2" will be released on April 16th. This is a sequel that takes place several years after the previous work, ``INDUSTRIA,'' and the story of the main character, ``Nora,'' who is trapped in a parallel world, is once again developed. A Japanese subtitled trailer is also being released along with the announcement.
