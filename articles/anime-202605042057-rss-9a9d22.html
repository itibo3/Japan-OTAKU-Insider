Details (EN)

The cumulative number of members of Akita Shoten's web manga sites Champion Cross and Yanchan Web has exceeded 2 million. To commemorate this, from today, May 4th, a peel-off advertisement will be launched at the Metro Promenade at Shinjuku Station in Tokyo and the Namba Panorama View Set at Namba Station in Osaka, where you can receive a special smartphone sticker on a first-come, first-served basis.
