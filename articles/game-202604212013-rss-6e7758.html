Details (EN)

UNCHAIN вҖӢвҖӢtoday released the PC browser game ``THIRD Mahjong'' on its official website. This work is a mahjong game for broadcasters that is equipped with a system called ``THIRD'' that allows viewers to intervene in the game in real time. Intervention actions such as ``hiding a hand tile'' and ``hiding a discarded tile'' are triggered by the tipped coin.
