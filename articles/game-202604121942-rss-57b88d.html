Yostar announced today (April 11, 2026) that it will hold a live broadcast to commemorate the half-anniversary of the company's ``StellaSola,'' and that the event ``A Fox's Shadow Dancing in the Dark'' will be held from after maintenance on April 16th until 4:59 on April 30th.
