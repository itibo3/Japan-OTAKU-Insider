Taito today announced that "Bubble Bobble Sugar Dungeon Boost" will be released for PS5/Switch on August 20, 2026. This work is an ACT game where you challenge the dungeons that change every time you step into them, aiming to go deeper into the dungeons while strengthening Bubbles. The PC version will be a "boosted version" with a free update scheduled to be released on the same day.
