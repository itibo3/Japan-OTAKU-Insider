The new future TV "ABEMA" is currently developing "Project PRISMation," a project that aims to create original anime works with next-generation anime creators. It has been announced that the original animated pilot film ``Poppin-Play Kitchen,'' which was released as the first film, has been nominated in the ``People's Telly'' category of the 47th Telly Awards, an international video advertising award.
