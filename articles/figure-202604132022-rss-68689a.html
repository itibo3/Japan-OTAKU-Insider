Summary

Kotobukiya has announced that the prototype of ``Sana Akahishi (fully equipped)'' from ``Mugen Encounter Megalo Maria'' will be on display at the 64th Shizuoka Hobby Show scheduled to be held at Twin Messe Shizuoka from May 13th to 17th.
