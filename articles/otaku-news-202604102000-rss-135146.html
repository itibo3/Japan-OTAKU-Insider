Details (EN)

Grammy Award-winning artist The Weeknd will appear at the "Crunchyroll Anime Awards 2026" to be held in Tokyo on May 23, 2026, and will serve as a presenter in the "Anime of the Year" category. He uses anime as a source of creativity, and created his debut work ``House of Balloons'' under the influence of ``Samurai Champloo.'' He has also deepened his relationships with Kenshi Yonezu, Tatsuki Fujimoto, and Riku Oseto.
