GigaQuests released the official version ``1.0'' of the turn-based strategy game ``ApesWarfare'' for PC/Mac on Steam on April 29, 2026. The price is 1,700 yen (tax included). The official version, released after about 6 months of early access, includes a ``campaign mode'' consisting of 35 missions in total, and a ``map editor'' that allows players to design their own battlefields.
