It has been announced that the animated feature film ``We Are Aliens'' directed by Kohei Kadowaki has been selected for the feature competition section of the ``Annecy International Animation Film Festival 2026''. This is director Kadowaki's first full-length film, and is a great achievement following his official submission to the 79th Cannes Film Festival Directors' Fortnight.
