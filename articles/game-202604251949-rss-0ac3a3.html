sakastudio has announced that it will conduct a closed beta test of ``moorestech'', which combines automated factory simulation with anime-like RPG elements, in June 2026. This work features a unique system that operates the factory using "gears" and "rotational energy" rather than electric wires. Recruitment of participants has begun via the official Discord.
