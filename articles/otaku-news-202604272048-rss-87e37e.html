Details (EN)

The 29th installment of the Detective Conan movie series, Detective Conan: Fallen Angel on the Highway, is currently in theaters. As of April 26, 2026, the box office revenue of this film has exceeded 7.99 billion yen, ranking it 93rd in the all-time box office revenue ranking according to Kogyo Tsushinsha. It entered the top 100 before Golden Week and is expected to become even more popular.
