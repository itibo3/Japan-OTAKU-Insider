Details (EN)

The awards for the ``30th Osamu Tezuka Cultural Award'' sponsored by Asahi Shimbun have been decided, and the Manga Grand Prize was won by Sei Kojima's ``Books Are Selling''. The presentation ceremony will be held at Yurakucho Asahi Hall in Tokyo on June 11, 2026, and a commemorative talk event will also be held on the day following the presentation ceremony.
