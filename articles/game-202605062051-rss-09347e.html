Details (EN)

Today's ``Hobonichi Indie PickUp!'' introduces ``Handmancers,'' a roguelike deck builder based on the three-way rock-paper-scissors game. It is an ambitious work that combines the simple and clear rules of rock-paper-scissors with the unique tactics of cards, the build construction using 13 slots of artifacts, and the accumulation of meta progression, making each run a fresh thought experiment.
