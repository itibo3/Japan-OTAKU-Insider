Details (EN)

CyberConnect2 has announced that it will release a smartphone version of the dramatic simulation RPG ``Battlefield Fuga'' in the summer of 2026. The game is optimized for smartphone operation, making it easier to play anywhere. Furthermore, in addition to the in-game benefits of the DX version for other models, the first to third DLC will be included. A trial play session is scheduled to be held at "Kansai Kemoket 11".
