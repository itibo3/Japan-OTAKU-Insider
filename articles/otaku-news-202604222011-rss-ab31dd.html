Details (EN)

We recommend watching the "Haikyu!!" series in the order of broadcast/release. You can enjoy the story in chronological order by watching the 1st season → 2nd season → 3rd season → 4th season → The movie version of ``Battle at the Garbage Dump''. If you watch OAD/OVA between seasons, you can immerse yourself more deeply in the world of the work, but even if you skip them, there will be no problem in understanding the main story.
