Torara Theater today began distributing the PC software ``Torara's Mahjong Classroom'' on Steam. This work is an experiential learning game where you can learn the rules of mahjong from scratch with the character ``Torara.'' The rules of mahjong are broken down as much as possible, allowing players to learn each one carefully at their own pace.
