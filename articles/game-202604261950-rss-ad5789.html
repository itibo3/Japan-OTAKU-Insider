Details (EN)

Aniplex announced today (April 25th) that the new game ``OCTOPinbs'' will be released on Steam on May 12th. This work is a werewolf-type action game in which the player disguises himself as a firefighter to catch an arsonist who sets fire by looking at his surroundings and identifying him. Up to 10 people can participate, and the feature is that the true identity is uncovered by observing behavior on the spot rather than through conversations.
