Details (EN)

Introducing the ``Kuroge Wagyu Mugen Beef Pot Bento'' by the long-established meat store Kakinayasu Honten, which comes with two new bento covers featuring the Flame Pillar and Anjuro Rengoku designs from the anime ``Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba.'' It comes with an original clear file that was popular last year, and reservations are now being accepted through the online reservation service "Kakiyoyaku."
