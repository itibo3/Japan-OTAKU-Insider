Details (EN)

It has been announced that Sanrio will launch its first game brand, ``Sanrio Games,'' which will be published in-house. In the fall of 2026, the first title, ``Sanrio Partyland,'' a party game for Nintendo Switch/NintendoSwitch 2, will be released simultaneously globally. Sanrio Games plans to release around 10 games over the next three years as a new point of contact between character IP and fans around the world.
