Details (EN)

The long-awaited latest film in the Mobile Suit Gundam: Flash of Hathaway series, ``Mobile Suit Gundam: Flash of Hathaway: The Witch of Circe,'' is currently in theaters. It has recently been revealed that the box office revenue for this film was 2.7 billion yen and the number of viewers exceeded 1.59 million. Furthermore, it has been decided that as a present for those who entered the final run of the first run, ``Cards designed by director Osamu Murase'' will be distributed from May 1st, the 14th week of release.
