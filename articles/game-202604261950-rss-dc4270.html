At the trial event ``Yusenkan'' sponsored by BilibiliGames, I experienced the new game ``Mandate Order,'' which is set in China during the Spring, Autumn, and Warring States periods. This work is an RTS centered around a nation-building simulation, and features a profound game design in which players are responsible for all elements of nation management, from resource gathering and production management to designing logistics networks and determining diplomatic policy.
