Details (EN)

It has been announced that the second season of the TV anime ``The Countryside Old Man Becomes a Kensei'', ``The Countryside Old Man Becomes a Kensei II'', will be broadcast on TV Asahi's national network ``IMAnimation W'' slot, BS Asahi, and AT-X from July 8, 2026. At the same time, the third PV using Unicorn's opening theme song "Kurenainoha" and the key visual depicting 13 characters, including additional characters that will appear in the second season, were released at the same time.
