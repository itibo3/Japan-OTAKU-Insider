Details (EN)

Becide will release ``Touhou Yukkuri Mountain'', a puzzle game in which Touhou Project's ``Yukkuri'' pile up, on Steam, Nintendo Switch, and Nintendo Switch 2 Edition in spring 2026. It can be played by up to 4 people, and it has also been decided that it will be playable at ``Tokyo Game Dungeon 12'' held during Golden Week.
