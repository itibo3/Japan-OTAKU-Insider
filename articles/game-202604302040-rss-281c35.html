Details (EN)

StormindGames has announced that Akira Yamaoka, a composer known for the ``Silent Hill'' series, will be in charge of the soundtrack for the new survival horror ``Remothered: Legacy of the Red Nun.'' This work is the final chapter of a horror trilogy inspired by the ``Clock Tower'' series, and depicts the truth about the ``Red Nun'' in a horrifying monastery.
