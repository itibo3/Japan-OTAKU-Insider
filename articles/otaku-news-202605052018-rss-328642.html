Details (EN)

``Moomin'' and Hilton Osaka 2nd floor ``Folk Kitchen'' will team up again for the ``Summer Sweets Buffet ~Moomin's Tropical Vacation~'', which will be held from June 4th to August 30th, 2026. This is a sweets buffet where you can enjoy the unique taste of summer, based on the story of the comic, ``The Moomin family and their friends travel from Moominvalley to a southern island and spend a vacation on the beach.''
