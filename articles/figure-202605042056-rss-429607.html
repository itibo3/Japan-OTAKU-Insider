Japanese title

「機甲創世記モスピーダ アナザージェネシス」より「トイライズ AFC-01X Ω レギオス オメガ」が再販決定。5月13日に予約開始

Summary

Takara Tomy's hobby label ``T-SPARK'' for high-end customers has announced that it will resell the finished product toy ``Toyrise AFC-01X Ω Regios Omega'' in late November. Pre-orders will begin on May 13th, and the price is 13,200 yen.
