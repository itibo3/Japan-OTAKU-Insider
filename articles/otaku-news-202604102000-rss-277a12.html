Details (EN)

It has been announced that the TV anime ``Book Lover's Ascendance: The Lord's Adopted Daughter'' included material that used generation AI in some cuts of the opening video. On April 10, 2026, an apology was announced on the work's official X (formerly Twitter) and official website as ``Report and Apology regarding the opening video,'' and a replacement will be implemented starting from the second episode, which will be broadcast tomorrow, April 11.
