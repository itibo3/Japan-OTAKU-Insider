The 5th anniversary collaboration of the anime “Jujutsu Kaisen” will be held at Diver City Tokyo Plaza from April 24th to May 17th, 2026. Goods with newly drawn illustrations will be sold, a bromide bonus will be given for every purchase of 2,000 yen or more at the pop-up store, and a collection card (8 types in total) will be given at random for purchases of 2,000 yen or more at the facility.
