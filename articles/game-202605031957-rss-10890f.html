Details (EN)

On May 3, 2026, Becide announced that it would release two types of "Toro Kuro" ``Anywhere Together'' pose stuffed animals on July 22, and began accepting reservations. The price is 2970 yen (tax included). This is a product commemorating the 27th anniversary of the game ``Dokodemo Issho,'' and its highlight is that you can change the pose by moving your arms and legs.
