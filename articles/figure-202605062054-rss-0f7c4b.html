Japanese title

「コードギアス」シリーズ20周年記念で拘束着や水着を纏った「C.C.」のスケールフィギュアが再販決定！

Summary

Union Creative will resell the figures "C.C. [Resale 26]" and "C.C. Swimsuit ver. [Resale 26]" in September and October, respectively. The price is 19,800 yen for "C.C. [Resale 26]" and 20,900 yen for "C.C. Swimsuit ver. [Resale 26]".
