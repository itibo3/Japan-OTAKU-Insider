Yukito Haruno's comic book ``My Cousin's Onee-san'' Volume 1 [AA] was released on the 8th. The back cover says, ``Asahi, a second year junior high school student, moves to the countryside where his mother's parents live for some reason. At the same time, my cousin Sayoko came home,'' and Obi's slogan was ``Summer in the countryside, in the heart of an adolescent boy.''
