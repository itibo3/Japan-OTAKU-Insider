Japanese title

「岸辺露伴は動かない 懺悔室」がNHKでテレビ初放送、ドラマ3エピソードも再放送

Summary

The movie "Rohan Kishibe Doesn't Move: Confession Room" based on the original work by Hirohiko Araki will be broadcast for the first time on NHK General TV at 4:10 p.m. on May 3. Some episodes of the drama series will also be rebroadcast.
