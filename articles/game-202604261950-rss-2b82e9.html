Details (EN)

Digitalis Publishing has announced that it will release the PC RPG ``Verita Tales: Witch of the Dark Castle'' in 2026. This is a game that digitally revives the feel of the game books ``Grail Quest'' and ``Sorcery'' that were popular in the 1980s, recreating the effect of writing numbers with a pencil and deciding your fate with dice. A scenario of approximately 300,000 characters has been prepared in which the player aims from the plague capital to the witch's castle.
