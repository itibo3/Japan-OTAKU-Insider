Japanese title

「サザエさん」「いじわるばあさん」初の電子書籍化、「サザエさん」は生誕80年

Summary

All 68 volumes of Machiko Hasegawa's ``Sazae-san'' and all 6 volumes of ``Ijiwaru Baasan'' have been made into e-books for the first time. Volumes 1 to 16 of "Sazae-san" and all volumes of "Ijiwaru Baasan" are being distributed today, April 22nd.
