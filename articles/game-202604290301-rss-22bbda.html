Details (EN)

Good Smile Company will be releasing the ``Uma Musume'' Fusaichi Pandora figure. Wearing the competition uniform "Assorted Cuteness", she has been sculpted into a three-dimensional figure with a dynamic pose and a confident expression. Details such as ribbons, frills, and pearl-painted hair are carefully sculpted. Optional parts for "Tehepero" face are also included. The price is 27,800 yen, and the release date is April 2027.
