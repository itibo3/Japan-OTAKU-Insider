The live event "Zombie Land Saga LIVE ~ Franchouchou Yume Ginga Festival ~" of the anime "Zombie Land Saga" series was held at Pia Arena MM in Kanagawa on April 4th and 5th. The live performance will feature Kaede Hondo as Sakura Minamoto, who plays members of the idol group Franchouchou in the story, Asami Tano as Saki Nikaido, Risa Taneda as Ai Mizuno, Maki Kawase as Junko Konno, Rika Kinugawa as Yugiri, and Mika Tanaka as Lily Hoshikawa. In this article, we will report on the April 5th performance.
