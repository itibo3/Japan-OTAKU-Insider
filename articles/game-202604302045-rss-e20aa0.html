Details (EN)

Cover, operated by Hololive, reports on the status of rights infringement response in 2025. There were 149 legal responses, including lawsuits and criminal cases, and 1,411 responses to SNS deletion. We are implementing thorough legal measures in five areas, including requesting disclosure of sender information for slander, shutting down malicious summary sites, responding to unfair use for financial gain, and coordinating with police in cases of intimidation.
