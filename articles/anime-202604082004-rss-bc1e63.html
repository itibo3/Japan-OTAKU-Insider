Details (EN)

The stage event for the TV anime "BLACK TORCH" based on the original work by Tsuyoshi Takakitori was held on March 28th at WHITE in "AnimeJapan 2026". The event was held at STAGE, and featured Ryota Suzuki who played Niro Agatsuma, Yoji Ueda who played Rakuji, Ayaka Senbongi who played Kazuka Kishimogami, Junya Enoki who played Reiji Kirihara, Junichi Suwabe who played Ryosuke Shiba, and Rena Ueda who played Mika Usami. Yoji Ueda also served as MC.
