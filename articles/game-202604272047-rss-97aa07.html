Koei Tecmo Games implemented an update for "Nio 3" on April 27, 2026. This time, features such as ``Battle Picture Scroll'', which allows you to enjoy highly difficult battles, and ``Stone of Difficulty'', which allows you to enhance the value and rarity of accessories in exchange for increasing the difficulty, have been implemented. Additionally, a 20% off sale is being held on Steam and PS Store until May 6th.
