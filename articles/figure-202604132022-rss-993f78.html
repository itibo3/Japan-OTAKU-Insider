Summary

Fine Mold has released a new product in its plastic model ``1/48 scale Japanese Army and Navy Aircraft'' series for the ``64th Shizuoka Hobby Show.'' The 64th Shizuoka Hobby Show will be held at Twin Messe Shizuoka from May 13th to May 17th.
