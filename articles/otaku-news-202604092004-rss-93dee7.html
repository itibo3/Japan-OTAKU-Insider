Details (EN)

The opening theme song for the spring anime ``Akane Banashi'', ``Hitonoshi'', is Keisuke Kuwata's first time writing an anime theme song at the age of 70. A passionate love letter from the production staff touched Kuwata's heart and led to this realization. The leading voice actress, Anna Nagase, has received rave reviews for her sincere attitude towards rakugo, such as sitting upright while dubbing.
