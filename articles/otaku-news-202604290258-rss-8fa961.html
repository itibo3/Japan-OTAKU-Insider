Details (EN)

An audiobook of the popular series ``Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba Novelization'' from the children's paperback book label ``Shueisha Mirai Bunko'' is now available, allowing you to enjoy ``Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba'' with your ears. From April 28, 2026, distribution of the first volume ``Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba Novelization ~Tanjiro and Nezuko, The Beginning of Fate~'' including an all-you-can-listen plan has started on ``audiobook.jp''.
