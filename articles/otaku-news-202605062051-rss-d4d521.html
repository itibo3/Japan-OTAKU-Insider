Details (EN)

It has been announced that the Kyushu Sanko Group's project ``Natsume's Book of Friends in Kumamoto 2026 ~Hitoyoshi/Kuma~'' in collaboration with the TV anime ``Natsume's Book of Friends'' will begin in July 2026. As part of the community contribution project, a special tour will be held around Hitoyoshi and Kuma, Kumamoto, which were the model locations for the anime, and a new illustration exclusive to this collaboration has also been released.
