Details (EN)

A collaboration project between the TV anime "Natsume's Book of Friends" based on the original work by Yuki Midorikawa and the Kyushu Sanko Group, "Natsume's Book of Friends in Kumamoto 2026 ~Hitoyoshi/Kuma~" will be held in July. A collaborative project between Kyushu Sanko Group and the theatrical animation ``To Fireflies in the Forest,'' also based on Midorikawa's original work, will be held at the same time.
