Details (EN)

It has been decided that Natsume Ono's ``GENTE ~The People of Ristorante~ [New Edition]'' will be published by Ota Publishing on May 26th. The series, which was originally published in three volumes, has been re-edited as two volumes with newly drawn covers. The first volume contains 8 stories and 1 extra story, and the second volume contains 7 stories and 3 extra stories.
