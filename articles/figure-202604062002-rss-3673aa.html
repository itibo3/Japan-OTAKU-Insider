Japanese title

「魔法戦隊マジレンジャー」より「ウーザフォン -MEMORIAL EDITION-」がプレバンにて4月6日16時より予約開始

Summary

Bandai will begin accepting reservations for the pretend toy "Uzaphone -MEMORIAL EDITION-" on the mail order site "Premium Bandai" from 4:00 pm on April 6th. Shipping is scheduled for September, and the price is 16,500 yen.
