Details (EN)

Neru Neko announced today that the PC game ``Hyakushakusama'' will be released on Steam in April. This work is an adventure game based on the urban legend of a tall woman, ``Hachishaku-sama,'' and depicts ``Hyakushaku-sama'' on a scale more than 12 times larger. Although the game is based on urban legends, there are few horror elements, making it a comical game.
