Details (EN)

Ami Shibata's picture book ``Tropical Boy Papuwa-kun'' will be released on July 9th. The release commemorative exhibition will be held at magmabooks in Toranomon, Tokyo from July 9th to August 9th, and will then travel to Osaka, Fukuoka, Sapporo, Nagoya, and Sendai. Author autograph sessions will also be held at each venue.
